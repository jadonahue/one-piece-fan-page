Meet The Straw Hat Crew

Luffy Zoro Nami Usopp Sanji Chopper Robin Franky Brook Jinbe

Luffy

Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Crew. His dream is to become to the Pirate King. He ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi fruit and is a rubber man. He cherishes his friends and loves to eat food!

Zoro

Zoro is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Crew. His dream is to be the greatest swordsman ever. He is a honarable friend and loves to take naps.

Nami

Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Crew. Her dream is to explore all the sea and chart the findings. She is a good friend but really enjoys money.

Usopp

Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Crew. His dream is to become a proud and brave warrior. He enjoys telling stories.

Sanji

Sanji is the Straw Hat Crew chef. His dream is to find the "All Blue Sea". He enjoys cooking and catering to women.

Chopper

Chopper is the Straw Hat doctor. His dream is to be the best doctor and have friends. He ate the Hito Hito no Mi fruit and is the emergency ration food.

Robin

Robin is the Straw Hat Archeologist. Her dream is to discover the ancient truth of the world. She ate the Hana Hana no Mi fruit and sprout extra body parts.

Franky

Franky is the Straw Hat Shipwright. His dream is to build the best ship ever. He also is a cyborg human with a lazer! SUUUUPPPPERRRR!

Brook

Brook is the Straw Hat Musician. He loves to sing and be with his friends. He also ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi fruit and survived his first death and is made of bones.

Jinbe

Jinbe is the Straw Hat Helmsman. He wants to be part of the future pirate kings crew. He is a fishman who knows how to fight.
